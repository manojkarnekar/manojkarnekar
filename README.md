##  About Me

I'm a Robotics Lead and researcher at the intersection of **Physical AI**, **autonomous systems**, and **robot intelligence**, with a background that spans theoretical physics (string theory, quantum computation) to real-world robot deployments across Japan, Dubai, and India.

I build robots that **perceive**, **decide**, and **act** in unstructured environments, from autonomous tugger bots carrying 1-ton loads on factory floors to lawnmowers with centimeter-level accuracy. I'm deeply interested in the next frontier: foundation models and world models that make robots genuinely general-purpose.

> *"Physics taught me how the universe works. Robotics lets me build a piece of it."*
